Project

Leads Bank (Sri lanka) PLC


View Source Code on GitHub

Technological advancements, particularly in artificial intelligence (AI), have revolutionized various industries by enhancing productivity, user experience, and efficiency. Leads Bank (Sri Lanka) PLC, facing challenges with its current human-dependent inquiry processing system, such as network errors, long waiting times, and poor communication, aims to leverage AI to improve customer satisfaction and retention. The bank plans to develop a text-based, well-trained AI chatbot capable of handling diverse queries and offering 24/7 assistance. This initiative will enhance customer experience, increase awareness of banking processes, and reduce time and costs, providing a comprehensive solution to current operational inefficiencies.

Project Details


Project Title Leads Bank (Sri lanka) PLC
Project Type Group Project
Project Aim The aim of this project is to develop a chatbot that would have the ability to communicate with the clients which would be trained to compare, examine, analyze as well as recognize questions based on given data, in order to provide the appropriate output when an inquiry is presented to the chatbot regarding any banking facility in Leads Bank.
Project Objectives
  • The main objective of this project is to provide a text-based, well-trained chatbot that can perform different patterns of queries and display the most appropriate reply.
  • To enhance customer experience with seamless service and increase customer satisfaction.
  • To elevate customer retention by providing 24/7 customer service assistance.
  • To increase the awareness of the customer regarding different banking processes and reduce time and cost.
