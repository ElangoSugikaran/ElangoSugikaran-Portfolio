Project

Raythos Aerospace


View Source Code on GitHub

Raythos Aerospace, a leader in innovative aircraft manufacturing, is embracing digital technology to revolutionize production and distribution. By developing an ASP.NET MVC-based online system, customers can seamlessly browse, customize, and purchase aircraft models. The project aims to enhance operational efficiency, leverage data analytics for informed decisions, and elevate customer experience with real-time tracking and communication, marking a new era of excellence in the aerospace industry.

Project Details


Project Title Raythos Aerospace
Project Methodology Spiral Methodology
Project Type Group Project
Project Aim To develop an innovative web platform that improves all aspect of the aircraft manufacturing process, from interacting with customers and customizing products to keeping an eye on production, managing inventories, and ensuring on-time delivery.
Project Objectives
  • User-Centered Interaction: Develop a secure, personalized user registration and login system prioritizing customer satisfaction and data protection.
  • Comprehensive Aircraft List: Create a dynamic, visually appealing aircraft catalogue with detailed information and transparent pricing.
  • Customized Aircraft Modifications: Integrate customization options for seating and interior design, enhancing customer experience.
  • Effortless and Secure Ordering: Streamline the ordering process from selection to secure online payment, ensuring a smooth customer journey.
  • Monitoring the Manufacturing Lifecycle: Implement a real-time system to track production lifecycles and provide order status updates.
  • Effective Inventory Control: Establish an inventory management system to monitor and optimize the supply of aircraft parts.
  • Coordinated Transportation and Arrangement: Integrate logistics functionalities to ensure secure, timely delivery of aircraft, enhancing customer satisfaction.
